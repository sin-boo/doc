[Multi-user Playit](https://github.com/sin-boo/multi-user-playit) is my personal clone of [Multi-User for Blender](https://gitlab.com/slumber/multi-user), the original addon by [Swann Martinez](https://gitlab.com/slumber) that lets multiple people work in the same Blender scene at once. I made this version to keep the same core experience but make setup simpler and connections more reliable when using playit.gg, so it is easier to get started and keep using. It is not the official Multi-User release — and despite the name, I am not affiliated with playit.gg, this is not a playit.gg product, and I do not make any profit from this addon or these docs. playit.gg is simply a third-party tunneling service used in the setup below. For the original addon, docs, and community, see the [Multi-User project on GitLab](https://gitlab.com/slumber/multi-user) or join the [official Discord](https://discord.gg/aBPvGws).

<Note>
A playit.gg **Premium** subscription is required for TCP tunnels.
</Note>

<Warning>
This clone is under active development and may contain bugs. It has been tested on Windows; use at your own risk.
</Warning>

## 4. Create your tunnel

<Columns cols={2}>
  <div>
    Sign in with your playit.gg account and follow the prompts until your agent is connected. Click **Create a tunnel**, then fill in the form:

    - **Name:** `blender` (or any name you prefer)
    - **Tunnel type:** TCP
    - **Port count:** 3
    - **Software description:** `blender for addon`
    - **Usage confirmation:** Confirmed
    - **Public endpoint:** Premium Network — closest datacenter to you
    - **Assign to agent:** Your connected agent
    - **Local IP:** `127.0.0.1`
    - **Local port:** `5555`
    - **Proxy protocol:** None

    Click **Next** to finish. On the **Tunnels** page, copy the address in the **orange box** at the top (for example, `protocols-yards.with.playit.plus:1209`) and share it with joiners.
  </div>

## 5. Download and install the addon

Go to the [Multi-user Playit releases on GitHub](https://github.com/sin-boo/multi-user-playit/releases) and download the latest version. In Blender, open **Edit → Preferences → Add-ons → Install** and select the downloaded file.

## 6. Host and join in Blender

<Columns cols={2}>
  <div>
    **To host:** Open **Playit.gg**, then in Blender open the **Multi-User** panel. Set port to `5555` and click **Host**.

    After the first setup, hosting is just open Playit.gg and press **Host** — you do not need to repeat the steps above.

    **To join:** Open the **Multi-User** panel, click **+** to add a server preset, paste the playit address from the orange box into the **IP** field, click **OK**, then **Connect**. Once saved, you can reconnect anytime.
  </div>
