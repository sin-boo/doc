## Create your account

Start by signing up on playit.gg:

1. Go to [playit.gg](https://playit.gg).
2. Click **Sign in** or **Get started**.
3. Follow the prompts to create your new account.

Once your account is set up, continue to [Create a playit.gg tunnel](/playit-tunnel) to configure your tunnel.
